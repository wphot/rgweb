RGMedia.Group is a digital product studio that specializes in three core disciplines: Digital Strategy, Product Development, and Marketing Solutions. We partner with ambitious teams and businesses to create successful digital products — from concept and strategy to engineering, launch, and growth. Our team operates across New York, Charlotte, and Medellin, combining global expertise with local insights to deliver measurable results for our clients.
RGMedia.Group has offices in three strategic locations: New York City, USA; Charlotte, North Carolina, USA; and Medellin, Colombia. This multi-city presence allows us to serve clients across North America and Latin America with localized support while maintaining a cohesive, collaborative team culture.
Project costs vary based on scope, complexity, and timeline. Our projects typically range from $5,000 for focused engagements to $50,000+ for comprehensive, full-package solutions. We provide transparent proposals after understanding your specific needs, so you always know exactly what you're investing in and the expected return.
Timelines depend on the project scope. A focused marketing campaign or strategy workshop can be delivered in 2-4 weeks. Full product development projects typically run 8-16 weeks from discovery to launch. We establish clear milestones and keep you updated throughout the process, ensuring on-time delivery without compromising quality.
We work with modern, industry-leading technologies tailored to each project's needs. Our tech stack includes React, Next.js, Node.js, TypeScript for web development; Swift and Kotlin for mobile; and a range of cloud platforms like AWS, Vercel, and Firebase. For marketing, we leverage data analytics platforms, SEO tools, and marketing automation systems to drive measurable growth.
